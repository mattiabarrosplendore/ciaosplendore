Mattia Barro is a Milan-based sound designer and artist working at the intersection of experimental music, institutional collaboration, and commercial sonic identity.

From gallery commissions to brand campaigns, he brings the same rigour: sound as a critical, deliberate act — not atmosphere, but argument.
